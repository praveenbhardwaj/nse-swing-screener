System Constitution
Trading Rules
These are not guidelines. They are the operating system of this strategy. Every rule protects capital, controls behavior, and preserves your edge.
▲ Entry ⬡ Risk ● Exit ◆ Mindset
▲ Entry#01
Wait for the Signal
Never enter before the screener emits BUY or STRONG BUY. Anticipation is speculation; patience is edge.
▲ Entry#02
Buy Within the Range
Execute only inside the published buy range. Chasing above range weakens the risk-reward setup.
▲ Entry#03
Minimum Score 75
Take trades only when the composite score is 75 or higher. Scores below 75 showed higher loss rates in backtests.
⬡ Risk#04
Honor Stop Loss Always
No averaging down, no manual overrides. If price violates SL, exit. Capital protection comes first.
⬡ Risk#05
Risk 1-2% Per Trade
Position size so a full SL hit costs only 1-2% of total capital.
⬡ Risk#06
Max 6 Open Positions
Beyond six trades, focus drops and correlation risk rises. Wait for exits before adding new exposure.
● Exit#07
Book at Target 1
Book at least 60% at T1 to lock gains and reduce emotional decision-making.
● Exit#08
Trail Stop After T1
Once T1 hits, move stop to breakeven on remaining quantity and protect the winner.
● Exit#09
Exit on Signal Deterioration
If score drops below 60 or MACD turns bearish, exit even before SL if momentum has degraded.
◆ Mindset#10
No Revenge Trading
A stopped trade is controlled risk, not failure. Do not re-enter impulsively.
◆ Mindset#11
Trust System Over News
Ignore noise from tips, social media, and headlines. Follow systematic signals.
◆ Mindset#12
Review Weekly, Not Daily
Avoid daily P&L obsession. Evaluate process quality weekly for stable execution.
